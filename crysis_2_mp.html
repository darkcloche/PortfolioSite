Sam Howels - Designer

Crysis 2 - Multiplayer DLC

Designer - PC, Xbox 360, PS3 (2011)

Having transitioned from the singleplayer team in Frankfurt to the UK studio, I was given the task of converting two campaign levels into multiplayer maps. Working directly with the level artists, I took areas from "Train To Catch" and "Masks Off" and adapted them to fit multiplayer - these were released in the "Decimation" map pack, and can be seen below.
