Sam Howels - Designer

Crysis 1 - Console Conversion

Designer - Xbox 360, PS3 (2011)

  • Having moved from the Frankfurt to Nottingham studio with large amounts of tools experience from previous CryENGINE projects, I was immediately tasked with bugfixing and polish for the game's most involved scripted events and cinematics that had fallen by the wayside. I also set up multiple workshops for team members who wanted to learn more about implementing work with the toolset.

  • During alpha and beta I was given ownership of the "Contact" and "Exodus" levels from the original game. This role involved balancing difficulty, polishing and adding elements to the levels to improve the gameplay experience, and fixing all level setup bugs reported during these phases. Some level elements were completely overhauled to clean up unreliable scripting and improve overall quality.

  • As well my own level responsibilities, I helped improve the implementation of multiple features across all levels. This included optimisation of AI counts when moving around within levels, creating more robust checkpointing logic to prevent spawn-death loops, achievement setup, and tutorial hint logic to prevent popups overlapping or being missed.
