Sam Howels - Designer

CryENGINE Game Development Blueprints

Creating Singleplayer Level Content in Sandbox

In 2014 I was contacted by Packt Publishing to contribute to a book on advanced CryENGINE guide, following from the success of the CryENGINE Cookbook. Using the publically available CryENGINE SDK, I contributed almost 100 pages to what would become the CryENGINE Game Development Blueprints. My section covers basic level blockout processes, Flow Graph (CryENGINE's visual scripting interface) and all related tools required to take a level from blockout to final quality, with best practices highlighted along the way.
